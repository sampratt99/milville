How to play: left-click to walk or use things. Right-click for more options. Arrow keys or WASD orbit and tilt the camera, scroll to zoom, middle-drag to look around, and click the compass to face north. Chop trees, mine rocks and net shrimps; cook your catch at the campfire; bury bones for Prayer. The guide by the fire can help.
