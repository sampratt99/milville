Install MilvilleOld School Adventure

Add the game to your device — launch it from your home screen and play even when you're offline.

Add to your iPhone / iPad

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add — Milville appears on your home screen.

Install from your browser

  1. On desktop Chrome or Edge: click the install icon (a monitor with a down-arrow) at the right end of the address bar.
  2. On Android: open the browser menu (⋮) and choose Install app / Add to Home screen.
